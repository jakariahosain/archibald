Rules

The REGC is 3 days competition divided into 2 qualifying days and 1 final.

 

  • 40 teams composed of 2 golfers.
  • Shotgun play
  • 4 balls aggregate game.
  • Each player plays his ball normally.
  • Both stableford scores are added.
  • Companies can register as many teams as they wish.
  • The best 10 teams will be invited to
    participate in the final

Covid safety measures & registration policy

  • This entire event is organized with the intention to be in full compliance with the COVID safety measures that will be in application on the day of the event.
  • All participants will have to follow all the club rules as well as the COVID safety measures.
  • The REGC will have the right to exclude anyone who doesn’t follow the rules or the safety measures.
  • The REGC will not be held responsible for the acts of its participants.

Cancellation & Refund

  • In case of a cancellation of the event due to local or national COVID measures, the participant will receive a full refund of his registration fees.
  • If the event can take place, no refund will be accepted.

FAQ

Can I register to more than one qualification day ?

Yes ! You can register to either 1 or 2 qualification days. Your best score will determine your ranking.

How is the score counted ?

It’s a 4 ball aggregate game. Everybody plays his ball. Both stableford scores are added up.

Can I play the final even if I didn’t qualify?

Sure. The best 20 teams are invited. The other teams can play the final by buying their greenfees.

Who wins prizes ?

The best 10 teams win an invitation to play in the final. Meaning per qualifying day : the best raw score of all categories + the 2 best net score per category. Other performances will also be rewarded with pretty cool gifts.

When do I have to pay ?

Your registration is confirmed once the payment has been made. The sooner you pay, the sooner you secure your spot.

Will I receive an invoice?

If you have completed your billing information, you sure will.

What happens if the event is cancelled ?

If the event is cancelled due to COVID reasons, you will receive a full refund of your registration fees. The event will be postponed to a later date.

How many teams can I register ?

A company can register as many teams as they want.

Can I play if i’m alone ?

You might. You’ll be put on a waiting list and contacted if a team needs a player.

Can I play if I’m a beginner ?

You’ll need to be at least 36 Hcp to be allowed to play.

Can I play with a pro ?

Yes but you can only have one single Hcp player per team.

Can I play if i’m not in the real estate sector ?

Yes but only if you’re invited by someone who’s in the sector. You must be at least 25 years old.
Please keep in mind that it’s a networking event.
